About

Second Cut started as a personal rail of pants we couldn't stop collecting, then couldn't stop passing on. We only sell one category, so every pair gets the attention a full store never has time for: waist and length measured by hand, condition checked seam by seam, nothing listed until it's ready to wear.

No dropshipping, no duplicates pretending to be one-offs. What you see in the grid is what's actually sitting on the rail, in one size, waiting for one person.

We keep the shop small on purpose. It's the only way to keep the tags honest.

How To Buy

  1. 01

    Find your pair

    Filter by brand, waist, or size, then open a listing to check the measurements and photos.

  2. 02

    Tap Buy On Instagram

    This opens a direct message to our Instagram. Nothing is charged here — the site is a catalogue, not a checkout.

  3. 03

    Tell us the name and your size

    Send the product name shown on the tag. We'll confirm it's still available and sort out payment and shipping over DM.

Pairs are one-of-one. First DM to confirm gets it — a pair marked SOLD on the site is no longer available.
